Prevention

The prevention of cyber-attacks is based on two main approaches. Equipment connected to computer systems must be designed to resist intrusion attempts. It is the responsibility of companies that offer this connected equipment. Security must be ensured by barriers capable of stopping intrusions. The equipment must not only communicate, but also be able to be sealed. A basic barrier is made up of identifiers and passwords, which must be managed very rigorously from the moment the machines are put into service.

B-SE consulting will be able to coach and advise in the technical field as well as in the field of employee awareness or preparation for the reaction of an attack , which are the most undervalued area of cybersecurity. Our strength lies in the intervention of the whole spectrum of cybersecurity.
